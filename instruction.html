1. Connecting.
Connect Ledger Nano S with firmware 1.6.x to computer via USB
2. Initialize your device.
Initialize device using 12-words seed phrase (help link) or just unlock Ledger using your PIN-code
3. Minter App
If you have already installed Minter App, just open it and click below "Open Console"
If you didn't, then click "Install Wallet to Ledger" and follow instructions
